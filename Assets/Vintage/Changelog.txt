Vintage 2.0
--------------------------
- 'Brightness / Contrast / Gamma', 'Hue / Saturation' and 'Film' become separate effects (now under 'Extra Effects' categories).

- More options to custimize shaders (see 'CONFIGURATION REGION' in shaders files).
- WebGL support.
- New effect 'Slumber'.
- Code refactoring.
- Small optimizations.
- Fixed a bug in the brightness in 'Sutro' effect.
- Documentation to PDF format.
